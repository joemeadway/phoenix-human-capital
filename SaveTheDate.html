Our Client Services

Database File Search

This involves a search of our database and utilisation of our industry network. Based on your brief, we are able to conduct a detailed search for relevant candidates, interview and asses them for their suitability and upon their consent details are presented to the client.

It is the most effective form of recruitment in relation to both cost and time.

Advertised Search

Advertised search can be used in conjunction with our other search methods depending on your requirements. We have negotiated advertising rates with a number of the key job boards and paper press and can pass on these savings to you. We manage the advert response, shortlist identified talent and provide you with a relevant selection of candidates to interview and employ.

Search & Selection

Search & Selection is the most focused way of identifying and attracting hard to find, business critical or specialist skill sets.

This method is provided on a retained basis and often targets candidates who may not be active in the job market. We undertake a systematic and thorough research process where we identify candidates often from within competitor companies who will be suitable for your brief. We discreetly approach these candidates on your behalf and provide you with a detailed report of all candidates approached in the search along with a shortlist of candidates interested in the position to interview.

Make the best use of our expertise

At Phoenix Human Capital we have the specialist industry knowledge, market experience, privileged insight and the key decision making contacts. We can help you determine who you should be speaking to and how to secure the best opportunity for you.

It's often the case that many positions go unadvertised by employers or that they are always interested in investing in the right people when they come along. Speak with us to make sure you have the full picture about the opportunities in the market and not just the ones that are on view in the public domain

Interview Coaching

We provide personalised and targeted interview coaching for all our candidates. Before any interview you will receive extensive documentation and coaching from one of our consultants to ensure you outperform the competition.

Salary Benchmarking

We have many years experience placing candidates into a variety of positions. Let us help you understand your market value when considering a career change. We can advise you to ensure you are not over or undervaluing yourself to ensure you get the job you want.

CV Writing & Presentation

It has taken you years to gain your experience. Let us help you make the most of it by guiding you through how to express yourself on paper. Your CV is the first opportunity to make a good impression, if it's not right there may not be a second chance.

Resignation & Counter Offer Coaching

Let us help ensure that your transition to a new employer is as smooth and stress free as possible. Let us guide you through the resignation process and ensure you are confident moving to that next position.

International Relocation Advice

In addition to working with locally based talent, Phoenix Human Capital have extensive experience in helping people relocate to Asia from abroad. Let our experienced team talk you through what to expect and how to ensure that your move to Asia is a great success.
